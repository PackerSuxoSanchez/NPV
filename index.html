Holiday Decoration Reminder


As a friendly reminder, holiday lighting and decorations may be displayed for a period of forty-five (45) days prior to and thirty (30) days after the holiday for which it is intended. Please ensure all December (and previous) holiday lighting and/or decorations on your home have been taken down (if any).
